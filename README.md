# the-engine-room

> These words are for mechanics. If you're talking to the driver, find a simpler word.

---

## The Idea

Building software systems should feel like Uber. The driver knows the route. The passenger just says where they want to go.

We have a manual for building systems - step by step, two recipes, smart defaults. Anyone can follow it. You don't need to understand the engine to use it.

But the moment we open our mouths, we walk people straight into the engine room.

This repository is a blacklist. Words that are perfectly fine between mechanics - but the second you're talking to a driver, a client, a business stakeholder, or anyone who just needs to get from A to B - stop. Find a simpler word.

If it's in here, replace it with something a human would say.
